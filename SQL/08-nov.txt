-> What is AWS database ?
AWS database refers to the database services offered by AWS a cloud computing platform by Amazon.
For unstructured or semi-structured data.
Amazon DynamoDB – Fully managed key-value and document database, great for high-scale applications with low latency.
Amazon DocumentDB – Managed database compatible with MongoDB.
Amazon Keyspaces (for Apache Cassandra) – Managed Cassandra-compatible database.

Cookies are small pieces of data that a website stores on your computer or device when you visit it. They help website remember
information about you.
How Cookies Work
You visit a website.
The website sends a small file (a cookie) to your browser.
Your browser stores it on your device.
When you visit the same website again, your browser sends the cookie back.
The website uses it to recognize you or remember your settings.
